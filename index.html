About

We are Nebulous Games, and we are a young game company currently making our first game, Astraphract.


Our Game

Our game is a space fantasy game that takes place in the future. Despite being in the future, the game features medieval undertones such as warring houses. It is a top-down view 2D game. The main character has a melee weapon and many abilities. It is on a quest to kill all of the enemies in the level.
